Projects

I have been programming my whole life, though for most of it I didn't realise I was, simply because I didn't understand what the term meant. However, these past five years I have been working extra hard to hone my experience and have be able to create multiple wonderful projects. Below are just a few!

  • Mingle App: Last year my friends and I made a Mingle App for those that are looking to find friends. The UI was designed off the UI of Mutual, and we even had our own swipping algorithm. The main difference however was that the users were on there just to find friends and could be post events if they are looking for someone to come with them and/or wanted to invite more people to join in on the fun!
  • whenisbest: Last year my friends and I also wanted to try our hand in recreating the 'whenisgood' website with increased features, newer design, and further integration without the use of so many links that the user would have to keep track of. This project was fun because we were able to learn a lot of things about integration across multiple platforms, but we didn't make it too far along before we lost interest haha
  • MyCHIPS: I also just finished my software emphasis capstone and made an app for our client who is looking to basically create the next bitcocin. This process was hard because we needed to learn a lot about his methodology since he didn't use a block chain, so he could acheive true decentralization. However, once we got the concepts down we were able to make him a state of the art app with real time money management systems.
